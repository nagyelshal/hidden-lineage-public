⚠️ Important Limitations & Disclaimers

📊 Statistical Considerations

ADMIXTURE models ancestry as proportions from reference populations. Results represent statistical similarity, not direct descent or cultural identity. Ancient populations are proxies for ancestral sources, not literal ancestors.

🧬 Reference Bias

Results depend on available ancient DNA samples, which may not represent complete ancestral diversity. Some populations and time periods have better coverage than others, potentially skewing estimates.

🏛️ Temporal Resolution

Ancient samples span thousands of years and may not capture rapid demographic changes. Population labels reflect archaeological associations, not proven cultural or linguistic continuities.
