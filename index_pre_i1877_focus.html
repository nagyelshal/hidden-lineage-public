Technical Note

This analysis uses ADMIXTURE with K=27 on a dataset of 16,772 ancient and modern samples. Higher K values reveal finer population structure but may also capture population-specific drift or noise. Results represent one detailed perspective on complex population history and should complement established ancestry methods. The "Unknown/Drift" component (19.76%) likely represents unsampled ancient populations or population-specific genetic drift.
