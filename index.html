📊 Understanding Different Ancestry Analysis Methods

🌍 23andMe Modern
"Where in the world does your DNA look most similar TODAY?"
  • K Value: K=20-50+
  • Reference: Living people from specific countries
  • Time Frame: Recent (last 500 years)
  • Resolution: Very high - specific countries
  • Purpose: Match to modern populations
Example Results:
• 25% Lebanese
• 18% Egyptian
• 12% Turkish
• 8% Italian
• 5% Scandinavian
🏺 23andMe Ancient
"What ancient groups contributed thousands of years ago?"
  • K Value: K=3-8
  • Reference: Ancient skeletons & broad groups
  • Time Frame: Deep (2,000-10,000+ years)
  • Resolution: Low - very broad categories
  • Purpose: General migration stories
Example Results:
• "Anatolian Farmers"
• "Steppe Pastoralists"
• "Western Hunter-Gatherers"
• Very vague percentages
🔬 Hidden Lineage
"Bridge ancient and modern with scientific precision"
  • K Value: K=3 to K=20
  • Reference: Both ancient + modern samples
  • Time Frame: Full spectrum (10,000 years to today)
  • Resolution: Scalable - from broad to specific
  • Purpose: Scientific discovery & education
